Recipe for Chocolate Brownies...

The steps are as follows:

  1. Let the eggs and butter sit out for 30 minutes to bring to room temperature.
  2. Preheat oven to 325°F.
  3. In a small bowl sift in the flour with the baking soda and salt then set aside.
  4. Add ½ of the chocolate chips (6 oz) and vanilla. Stir until smooth and shiny.
  5. Transfer the chocolate mixture into a large bowl.
  6. Add eggs one at a time beating well after each addition.
  7. Stir in remaining chocolate chips and nuts if desired.
  8. Bake at 325°F for 30-35 minutes.
  9. Wow! Delicious brownies are ready!
